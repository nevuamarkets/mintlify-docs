## For a watchlist

- Click the "Manage Alerts" button on the action bar of every watchlist
- The revealed interface allows you to view, edit, start/stop, or delete existing alerts

## Across all watchlists

- Click the "Manage Alerts" sidebar button
- This page shows **all alerts** across **all watchlists** and allows you to view, edit, start/stop, or delete existing alerts
