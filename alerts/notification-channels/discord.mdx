## How to connect your Discord server

To receive notifications in Discord, you'll need to create a webhook for your channel. Here's how:

1. Click the ⚙️ '**Edit channel**' icon next to the channel you want to receive notifications in
2. Select '**Integrations**' → '**Create Webhook**' 
3. Click on the newly created webhook and then click '**Copy Webhook URL**'

## Setting up your alerts

Once you have your webhook URL, you can enable Discord notifications. 

When creating or editing an alert, select Discord as a notification channel and paste your webhook URL in the text box that appears.
