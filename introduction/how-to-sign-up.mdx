---
title: 'How to Sign Up'
---

To get started, simply click the **Log In** button. If you don't have an account yet, **one will automatically be created for you** during the login process.

You have two login options:

- **Social Login with Google**: Sign in quickly using your Google account
- **Email Login**: A secure login link will be sent to your email address, valid for one-time use within 15 minutes
